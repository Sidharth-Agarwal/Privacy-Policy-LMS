# Privacy Policy
**Last updated:** February 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to our mobile application and describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## 1. Information Collection and Use
We collect specific information required for account management, order processing, and app functionality:

### A. Personal Identity & Contact Data
- **Identity:** First Name, Last Name
- **Contact:** Email Address, Phone Number
- **Profile Image:** User profile picture (optional upload)

### B. Transaction & Order Information
- Shipping Address / Delivery Address
- Order History and Purchase Records
- Payment Method (we do not store full card details)
- Wallet Balance and Transaction History

### C. App Activity Data
- **Browsing & Search:** Products viewed, searched, or added to cart
- **Preferences:** Saved/favorited items and wishlist data

> **Note:** This App does **not** collect or process sensitive personal data such as Religion, Caste, Blood Group, Gender, Nationality, or Parental/Guardian details.

## 2. App Permissions
- **Camera & Gallery (Storage):** Required only for uploading profile pictures
- **Internet Access:** Required to fetch real-time product and order data from secure servers
- **Location (Optional):** Used only to assist with accurate delivery address entry

## 3. Data Storage
All data is securely stored using **Google Firebase** services:
- **Authentication:** Firebase Authentication manages login credentials
- **Database:** Cloud Firestore stores order and user records with strict security rules
- **Media:** Firebase Storage hosts profile pictures and product images

## 4. Third-Party Services
The app uses these third-party services for analytics and crash reporting:
- [Google Play Services](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- [Google Analytics for Firebase](https://firebase.google.com/policies/analytics)

## 5. Data Retention & Deletion
Your data is retained while your account remains active. To delete your account or request data removal, contact our support team or use the in-app account deletion feature.

## 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Check this page for changes.
